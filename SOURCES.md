# Moodboard asset sources

High-resolution images were downloaded for:

- **Floral collection**: **5 studio-style images per flower**, ideally with black/dark backgrounds. All from Unsplash (Unsplash License) unless noted.
  - **Pamposh (Lotus)**: Sacred lotus (Nelumbo) style only — studio shots on black/dark background; no water lilies.
  - **Yemberzal (Narcissus)**: Studio and black-background narcissus/daffodil.
  - **Kong Posh (Saffron)**: Crocus/saffron flowers (purple crocus bloom).
  - **Gulab (Red rose)**: Red rose studio shots on black background.
  - **Gul-e-Lala (Tulip)**: Tulip studio shots, black/dark background where possible.
- **Handicrafts**: Kashmiri patterns (Sozni/Kani embroidery, cashmere/pashmina shawls, Amlikar, Jamewar, boteh) — from Wikimedia Commons (CC BY-SA 4.0, CC0) and Met Museum (CC0/open access).
